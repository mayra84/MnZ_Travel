Meet the Creators

Learn more about the creators of Movie Travel and what led them to making this application

Mayra Estrella

Hello world! I am the 'M' in "MNZ". Welcome to our app! Our goal here at MNZ Corporation is to connect you to your favorite films and their filming locations, without ever leaving your home. Have fun exploring!

Noah Dirck

What can I say? I'm the typical guy who studied abroad one semester and fell in love with traveling, more specifically-- Spain. I am also a film connoisseur so I thought "Hey, let's Resse's-Peanut-Butter this thang and make an app combining my two favorite things". This is the product of my two passions. Enjoy!

Zaria McBurrows

I was inspired by going to San Francisco and trying to find a place from a TV show and it was time consuming, so an application for it would make it a lot easier.
